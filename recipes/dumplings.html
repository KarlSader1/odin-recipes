Dumplings

Description

This is the final product of the dumplings we are about to make. Stunning, isn't it? It is a recipe that produces the perfect flavor and texture.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Flour
  • 2 tsp. of Baking powder
  • 2 tsp. of Sugar
  • 1/2 tsp. of Salt
  • 1/2 cup of Milk
  • 1 tbsp. Margarine

Steps

  1. Stir together flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Cut in butter until mixture is crumbly.
  3. Stir in milk and mix until a batter forms that is thick enough to be scooped with a spoon.
  4. Allow batter to rest for 3 to 5 minutes.
  5. Drop batter by spoonfuls into boiling stew or soup.
  6. Cover and simmer without lifting the lid for 15 minutes.
