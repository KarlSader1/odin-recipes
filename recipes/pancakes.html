Pancakes

Description

This is the final product of the pancake we are about to make. Stunning, isn't it? It is a recipe that produces the perfect amount of sweetness and texture.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of Flour
  • 1 tbsp. of Baking powder
  • 2 tbsp. of Sugar
  • 2 tsp. of Salt
  • 3 cups of Milk
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 tbsp. of Butter

Steps

  1. Sift the dry ingredients together (flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt)
  2. Make a well, then add the wet ingredients (eggs, milk, butter)
  3. Scoop the batter onto a hot griddle or pan
  4. Cook for two to three minutes, then flip
  5. Continue cooking until brown on both sides
